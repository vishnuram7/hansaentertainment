The Hansa Foundation

A Name Built
on Integrity

Hansa is a strong household name in India, built on Integrity and Trust for over three decades. In the South, it is known for content production through its associate entity Hansa Vision for 15 years between 1989 and 2004. Hansa Vision had produced & marketed over 4000 hours of Television Programming. The company had pioneered pathbreaking format changes in South-Indian Television, like starting the 5-day soap opera format in Tamil, marketing Hindi tele-serials like Ramayan, Vikram Aur Betaal, Chandrakantha etc. Hansa Vision also brought several ODI Cricket matches from Australia through Satellite transmission for the first time in the country. They also hosted the first ever Yanni Concert LIVE held in the backdrop of Taj Mahal, Agra and produced & broadcasted a series of Michael Jackson shows, prior to his India visit. Several superhit films were also acquired in multiple languages and broadcast across Doordarshan and Satellite Networks by Hansa Vision.

With this rich legacy behind us and keeping with us the pioneering spirit of adapting to newer formats, Hansa Entertainment is now set to build upon the Hansa legacy in the context of producing content for multiple screens for a mobile-first audience. This entity is being led by the younger generation of the Swamy family who have enormous interest in this space. The founders of Hansa Entertainment, Sruti Swamy and Siddharth Swamy have both imbibed the group culture having worked with their father, Mr. Srinivasan K Swamy (Exec-Group Chairman, RK Swamy-Hansa) and uncle, Mr. Narasimhan K Swamy (MD & Group CEO Rk Swamy-Hansa) for several years. Sruti has a creative bent of mind having done her Masters in Interior Architecture & Design from Milan. She is also a PG in Business Management from the Indian School of Business. Siddharth has a deep interest in film production and content creation. He studied both his Under Graduate and Post Graduate courses overseas and is a Data Scientist from Imperial College London, thus bringing with him newer perspectives.

Hansa Entertainment therefore operates at the intersection of creativity and strategic scale in storytelling with the speed, data-intelligence and platform-fluency for modern story systems.
