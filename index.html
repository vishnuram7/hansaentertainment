The Architect

Core Team

Sujatha Narayanan
Chief Executive Officer

A creative & production leader with over 23 years of experience spanning script and content ideation, film and digital production, media programming, script design and creative direction across radio, television, OTT, and cinema. Having worked across all four South Indian markets and Mumbai, she brings a rare blend of business vision and creative production expertise to storytelling. At Hansa, she leads the company’s strategic, business and creative direction, shaping a new generation of premium content that cuts across formats for global audiences.

Sruti Swamy
Director

A design leader and entrepreneur with extensive experience in architecture, spatial design, and retail branding across large-scale commercial and experiential projects, Sruti beings her visual aesthetics to the realm of storytelling. With global business training and a track record of building impactful businesses, she brings a strong strategic and macro perspective to the board. Recognizing the transformative potential of storytelling and experience-led brands, she supports Hansa’s vision for growth in the entertainment space.

Ramthilak M
Growth & Strategy Head

A growth strategist and marketer with over a decade of experience driving scalable audience and revenue growth across media, tech, and consumer platforms. Known for combining data-driven strategy with creative execution, he specializes in building high-impact launches, digital ecosystems, and monetization frameworks. At Hansa, he focuses on shaping the growth engine for a global, digital-first era.

Barath Neelakandan
Contributing Content Lead

A filmmaker, actor, and writer with over a decade of experience in directing, screenwriting, and executive production. Barath is known for his out-of-the-box debut film K-13. He brings a strong storytelling sensibility combined with deep expertise in end-to-end content development. At Hansa, he contributes to vertical drama stories and shapes compelling narratives across formats for contemporary audiences.

Aditi Ravindranath
Films & OTT Business

A seasoned media professional with over 20 years of experience across production, creative development, sales, marketing, and exhibition in the Tamil film industry. She has held key roles as Executive Producer, Creative Producer, Head of Sales and Marketing and Project Designer on major films and series, including Mersal, Navarasa (Netflix), Vendhu Thanindhathu Kaadu, Kochadaiyaan, and most recently Aaromaley, where she served as Creative Producer, with the film going on to receive critical acclaim post release.
